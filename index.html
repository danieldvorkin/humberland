Welcome to Humberland Montessori Academy

Mission Statement

Humberland Montessori is committed in providing an excellent education in a secure, diverse, warm-hearted and creative environment. Our mission is for each child to be academically driven, motivated to always try their best, supported in all that they do, encouraged to try new things, and physically active.

School Philosophy

Our goal at Humberland Montessori Academy (HMA) is to create a pleasant and nurturing environment where the child is able to reach his/her full potential. We believe education is the foundation for life. The education a child receives at an early age is one of the most important factors that will determine his or her future. Parents, students, and teaching staff share equally in the achievement of our goals.

Why Choose HMA?

  • Provide an enriched experience and create a love of learning
  • Develop a child’s full potential
  • Guide children in their development and stimulate growth and socialization
  • Ensure the children have a solid understanding of the fundamentals of Math and Language
  • Give children the opportunity to explore science and technology and gain an appreciation for discovery and invention
  • Develop creative thought and logic abilities
  • Help children explore their natural interests and skills
  • We are a dedicated team of loving and nurturing Montessori trained teachers
